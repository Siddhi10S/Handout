About Us

HAND-OUT is a website or platform for all the people who need books, stationary, food, clothes and money for education or person who cannot afford it. This also for people who want to give away their unused or used books, clothes ,stationary that are no longer required to them. The system has the facility to donate money as well. The donation can be made to an individual or to the NGOs who needs it . The proposed application would make majority of the people to get involved in donations and would eliminate wastage and promote reusability It will help to reduces the hunger deaths and fulfill the NGOs motto . It will create a positive vibe for donors by donating food , clothes , books and money. At last both the user and society gets benefited.
